HAPPY TO HELP YOU
MYMAILS is there to guide you, on how to go about it.....

SEARCH

This option allows you to search anything you feel like. Just type into the bar, the stuff you are looking for.

MANAGE MY ACCOUNTS

This option allows you to manage your account. At the right-top most corner of your screen you will see your user_id. All you need to do is just to click on it.
      

--VIEW MY PROFILE

Displays you the respective profile of yours. It also allows to update certain changes in your profile.
      

--PRIVACY

In this section , certain policies are given. Also it is mentioned that how can you protect your account from other users.

COMPOSE

This allows you to create an e_mail that you want to deliver .
      

--ATTACH

The button in the form of + allows you to attach files with the e_mail.
      

--SEND

After creating your e_mail ,when you are set to deliver it , all you need to do is just to click on "send".

INBOX

With a click on "inbox" , you can view, what all mails you have received.

SENT MAIL

With a click on "sent mail" , you can view, what all mails you have sent.

DRAFTS

The mails which could not be at that moment are stored as drafts. With a click on drafts you can view mails stored as drafts.

ALL MAILS

This will contain the mails which are stored as "inbox", "sent mail", "drafts".

HANGOUTS

This will display you the list of people whom you have contacted more than two times. They are added to your "hangouts" circle.

CHATS

This allows you to set a conversation between yourself and anybody you select from your hangouts.
